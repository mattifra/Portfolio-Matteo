Lacerba.io is an online startup community focused on Italian market. At Lacerba we teach people how to convert an idea into reality building web applications with Ruby on Rails learning the lean startup approach and the basic marketing and design tactics in order to successfully start a new startup.

Our courses will walk the students through the whole process of developing Startup Projects step-by-step including mentorship interview with italian founders.
